DATA COLLECTION MOBILE APPs

The Ethiopian Business Climate Monitoring System (EBCMS) Mobile App is a user-friendly tool designed to facilitate real-time data collection and reporting on various business climate indicators. This app empowers citizens, businesses, and government officials to contribute to the monitoring process directly from their mobile devices.

Key Features

Intuitive Interface

Simple and easy-to-navigate interface ensures a seamless user experience

Real-Time Data Collection

Submit data on various indicators including business registration times and tax compliance

Geolocation Tracking

Pinpoint location of data submissions for accurate analysis and mapping

Offline Functionality

Collect data even in areas with limited connectivity

Data Validation

Built-in validation checks ensure data accuracy and consistency

Secure Data Transmission

Data transmitted securely using encryption protocols

By leveraging the power of mobile technology, the EBCMS Mobile App aims to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of data collection, ultimately leading to more informed decision-making and improved business climate policies.
