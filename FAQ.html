Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

1. What is the Ethiopian Business Climate Monitoring System (EBCMS)?

+

The EBCMS is a comprehensive platform designed to track and analyze key indicators that impact the business environment in Ethiopia. It provides real-time data and insights to policymakers, investors, and businesses.

2. Who developed the EBCMS?

+

The EBCMS was developed by Dart Digital Technologies Plc, in collaboration with Giz.

3. What is the purpose of the EBCMS?

+

The primary purpose of the EBCMS is to:

  • Improve the understanding of the business climate in Ethiopia
  • Identify challenges and opportunities
  • Inform policy decisions and business strategies
  • Attract domestic and foreign investment

Data and Indicators

4. What kind of data does the EBCMS collect?

+

The EBCMS collects a wide range of data on various business climate indicators, including:

  • Ease of doing business
  • Investment climate
  • Regulatory environment
  • Infrastructure development
  • Access to finance
  • Labor market conditions
  • Trade policies
