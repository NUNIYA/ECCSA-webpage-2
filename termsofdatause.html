TERMS OF DATA USE

Introduction

The Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce and Sectoral Association (ECCSA) is committed to promoting a conducive business environment in Ethiopia. As part of this commitment, ECCSA collects and analyzes data related to various business climate indicators. This document outlines the terms of use for this data.

Data Usage Policy

1. Purpose of Data:

  • Internal Use: ECCSA may use the data for internal analysis, research, and policy advocacy.
  • External Sharing: ECCSA may share aggregated and anonymized data with relevant stakeholders, such as government agencies, international organizations, and private sector entities, to promote evidence-based policymaking and business development.

2. Data Collection Methods

Data may be collected through a variety of methods, including:

  • Surveys and questionnaires
  • Interviews
  • Focus groups
  • Document analysis
  • Secondary data sources (e.g., government reports, international databases)

3. Data Confidentiality:

  • Confidentiality: ECCSA will treat all confidential and proprietary information with the utmost care.
  • Anonymization: Data will be anonymized and aggregated to protect the privacy of individuals and businesses.
  • Security: ECCSA will implement appropriate security measures to protect the data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Accuracy:

  • Data Quality: ECCSA will strive to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the data collected.
  • Data Validation: Data will be validated and cross-checked to minimize errors.
  • Data Updates: ECCSA will regularly update the data to reflect changes in the business climate.

5. Data Sharing:

  • Data Sharing Agreements: ECCSA may enter into data sharing agreements with other organizations, subject to appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
  • Data Licensing: In certain cases, ECCSA may license the use of data to third parties, subject to specific terms and conditions.

6. Intellectual Property Rights:

  • Ownership: ECCSA retains ownership of the data and any intellectual property rights associated with it.
  • Usage Rights: Users of the data may use it for non-commercial purposes, subject to the terms of this agreement.

7. Disclaimer of Liability:

  • Data Accuracy: ECCSA makes no warranties, express or implied, regarding the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of the data.
  • Liability Limitation: ECCSA shall not be liable for any damages arising from the use or misuse of the data.

8. Data Retention

Data will be retained for a period of [specify time period, e.g., 5 years] or as required by law. After this period, data will be securely destroyed.

9. Consent

By participating in the data collection process, respondents consent to the use of their data for the purposes outlined in this document.

10. Changes to the Terms of Data Use

ECSA reserves the right to modify these terms of data use at any time. Any changes will be communicated to affected parties.
